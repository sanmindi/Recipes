1 small onion
2 tomatoes
2 spoons of menthugunda
1 small spoon or lesser of garam masala
little turmeric
salt to taste
ginger garlic paste

Fry onions until golden brown
add tomatoes
add salt
stir fry fr sometime
Add little ginger garlic paste(less than a spoon)
add menthugunda+little water
fry for sometime
add 1 1/2 glasses of water
add little turmeric+garam masala
