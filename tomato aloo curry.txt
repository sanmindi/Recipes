Boil 4 potatoes in cooker by cutting into two pieces.
3 whistles:1 whistles in high flame and 2 in medium flame.

Chop three tomatoes and 4 green chillies.
Let the oil get heated.
Add little of zeera and then smashed ginger and curry leaves. Fry for sometime.
Now add tomato and greenchilli,put salt and after little bit of frying,add half spoon turmeric,little of red chilli powsder,half of zeera powder . Fry for a long time until the tomatoes stick to the pan.
Then put water and once it comes to boil smash the potatoes into it.
